Binayak Care Hospital

"Healing with Heart, Serving with Integrity."

At Binayak Care Hospital, our journey began in 2025 AD / 2082 BS with a vision: "To bring compassionate, high-quality healthcare within everyone’s reach."

Nestled in the heart of the community, our hospital was founded on the belief that affordability should never come at the cost of excellence. From day one, we've committed ourselves to delivering world-class medical care that rivals top-tier private hospitals — without burdening our patients financially.

With 150+ beds, a dedicated team of 50+ experienced doctors, and a NABH accreditation that ensures we meet the highest standards in medical services, we’ve built more than just a hospital, we’ve built a place where care meets trust.

Whether it's a sudden emergency at midnight or a long-term health concern, we’re here for you 24/7, always ready, always caring.
